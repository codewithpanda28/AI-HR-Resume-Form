What Information We Collect

Plain Language Summary

We collect only the information you provide when submitting your application through our form. This includes your contact details, professional information, resume file, and any preferences you select.

Information You Provide Directly

Personal Information

  • • Full name
  • • Email address
  • • Phone number
  • • Location (city, state)

Professional Details

  • • Job title and experience level
  • • Skills and competencies
  • • Professional summary
  • • Resume file or LinkedIn profile

Application Preferences

  • • Preferred job type
  • • Expected salary range
  • • Communication preferences
  • • Notification settings

Technical Information

  • • IP address (for security)
  • • Browser type and version
  • • Device information
  • • Submission timestamp

How We Use Your Information

Plain Language Summary

We use your information solely for recruitment purposes: reviewing your application, contacting you about opportunities, and improving our hiring process. We never sell your data or use it for unrelated marketing.

Application Processing

Review your qualifications, match you with suitable positions, and facilitate the hiring process with relevant employers.

Communication

Send you updates about your application status, interview invitations, and relevant job opportunities based on your preferences.

Service Improvement

Analyze application patterns to improve our platform, enhance user experience, and optimize our matching algorithms.

Legal Compliance

Maintain records as required by employment law, respond to legal requests, and ensure compliance with data protection regulations.

How We Store and Protect Your Data

Plain Language Summary

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures. We keep your information only as long as necessary for recruitment purposes, typically 2 years unless you request earlier deletion.

Security Measures

  • 256-bit SSL encryption in transit
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

Data Retention

  • Active applications: Until hiring decision
  • Talent pool: Up to 2 years
  • Legal requirements: As mandated
  • Deletion upon request: Within 30 days

When We Share Your Information

Plain Language Summary

We share your information only with potential employers for specific job opportunities, trusted service providers who help us operate our platform, and when required by law. We never sell your data to third parties.

With Your Consent

We share your application with employers only for positions you've applied to or expressed interest in.

Service Providers

Trusted partners who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics, email services) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

We Never Do This

  • • Sell your data to third parties
  • • Share information for unrelated marketing
  • • Provide data to data brokers
  • • Use your information for advertising purposes
