Technical Support

If you're having trouble uploading your resume, try these solutions:

  • Ensure your file is in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format
  • Check that your file size is under 5MB
  • Try refreshing the page and uploading again
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

If the problem persists, you can provide your LinkedIn profile URL as an alternative.

Our form is optimized for mobile devices. If you're experiencing issues:

  • Ensure you're using an updated browser
  • Try switching from mobile data to WiFi or vice versa
  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Try rotating your device to landscape mode for file uploads
  • Use the LinkedIn URL option if file upload fails

For the best mobile experience, we recommend using Chrome or Safari browsers.

Resume Portal works best with modern browsers. We recommend:

  • Chrome: Version 90 or newer
  • Firefox: Version 88 or newer
  • Safari: Version 14 or newer
  • Edge: Version 90 or newer

If you're using an older browser, please update to the latest version. Internet Explorer is not supported.

Application Process

To complete your application, you'll need:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone, and location
  • Professional Details: Job title, experience level, and key skills
  • Resume: PDF, DOC, or DOCX file (or LinkedIn profile URL)
  • Preferences: Job type and salary expectations (optional)

The entire process typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete.

Currently, the application form doesn't save progress automatically. However:

  • The form is designed to be completed in one session (5-10 minutes)
  • Your browser may retain some information if you navigate away briefly
  • We recommend completing the application when you have all required information ready

If you need to step away, consider preparing your information in a separate document first.

After submitting your application:

  1. You'll receive an immediate confirmation with your application ID
  2. Our team will review your submission within 2-3 business days
  3. You'll receive email updates about your application status
  4. If selected, we'll contact you to schedule an interview

You can track your application status using the Status Tracker.

Once submitted, applications cannot be edited directly. However, you can:

  • Contact our support team with updates or corrections
  • Submit additional documents via email if requested
  • Provide updated information during the interview process

For significant changes, please contact us at support@resumeportal.com with your application ID.

Privacy & Security

We take data protection seriously and implement multiple security measures:

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Information is stored on protected servers
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access applications
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security reviews and updates

Read our full Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Access to your application is strictly limited to:

  • HR team members involved in the recruitment process
  • Hiring managers for relevant positions
  • Authorized system administrators for technical support

We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

Yes, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. You can:

  • Email us at privacy@resumeportal.com
  • Include your application ID and deletion request
  • We'll process your request within 30 days
  • Some information may be retained for legal compliance

Note that deletion may affect our ability to consider you for future opportunities.

Account Management

No account creation is required! Our streamlined process allows you to:

  • Submit applications directly without registration
  • Track your application using the provided ID
  • Receive updates via email

This approach respects your time while maintaining security and functionality.

After submitting your application, you can track its progress by:

  • Using your application ID on our Status Tracker
  • Checking email updates sent to your registered address
  • Monitoring for status changes in real-time

Keep your application ID safe - you'll need it to access status updates.

If you're not receiving our emails, please check:

  • Spam/Junk folder: Our emails might be filtered
  • Email address: Ensure you provided the correct address
  • Email filters: Check if you have filters blocking our domain
  • Inbox capacity: Ensure your inbox isn't full

Add noreply@resumeportal.com to your contacts to ensure delivery.
